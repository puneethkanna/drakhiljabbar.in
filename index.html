About

Dr. M. A. Jabbar is a distinguished academician and researcher in Computer Science and Engineering, specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. He currently serves as Professor and Head of the Department of CSE (AI & ML) at Vardhaman College of Engineering, Hyderabad.

He is recognized as one of the World’s Top 2% Scientists by Stanford University and Elsevier.
